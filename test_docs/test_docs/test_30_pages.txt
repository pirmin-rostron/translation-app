DEFINITIONS - PAGE 1

Agreement means this contract between the Customer and the Provider.
Customer refers to the individual or entity receiving the services.
Policy refers to the governing operational rules of the Provider.
Service means the translation and document processing services provided hereunder.
The Customer must comply with all applicable policies at all times.
The Provider reserves the right to modify the service terms with reasonable notice.

TERMINATION - PAGE 2

This Agreement shall terminate immediately upon breach of its terms.
The contract shall terminate immediately if payment obligations are not fulfilled.
The agreement will end immediately in the event of non-compliance.
Either party may terminate this agreement with sixty days written notice.
Upon termination the Customer shall immediately cease use of all services.
The Provider shall retain all data for ninety days following termination.

OBLIGATIONS - PAGE 3

The Customer must comply with all applicable policies and requirements.
The Customer is required to adhere to all relevant policies.
The Customer shall follow all governing policies and procedures.
The Provider shall deliver services in accordance with the agreed levels.
Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality regarding proprietary information.
The Customer agrees to provide accurate information for service delivery.

LIABILITY - PAGE 4

The Provider shall not be liable for indirect damages under any circumstances.
The Provider is not responsible for any indirect losses arising from interruptions.
The Customer agrees to indemnify the Provider against all third party claims.
Neither party shall be liable for delays caused by force majeure events.
The maximum liability shall not exceed the fees paid in the preceding month.
The Customer assumes all risk associated with the use of translated documents.

CONFIDENTIALITY - PAGE 5

Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding proprietary information.
The Customer shall not disclose the terms of this agreement to any third party.
The Provider agrees to handle all customer documents with the highest discretion.
Confidential information shall not include publicly available information.
The confidentiality obligations shall survive termination for five years.
Each party shall implement reasonable security measures to protect information.
